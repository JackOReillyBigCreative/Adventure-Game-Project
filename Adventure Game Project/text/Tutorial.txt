UPPERCASE text passes automatically and is spoken by machines.

lowercase text passes after pressing enter.

Combat is turn based, you take a turn and then the enemy takes their turn.
During boss fights it will be indicated for what attack the enemy is going to use, allowing you to prepare yourself for the next attack.

The ONLY WAY to restore your fuel (Health) is by fighting enemies and winning.

Stronger enemies with more mass will restore more fuel, while weaker enemies with less mass will restore less hp.

There are no incorrect paths, they will all lead further down through hell.


Press any key when you are ready to return to the menu.
